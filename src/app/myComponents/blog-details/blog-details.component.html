High in antioxidants

Antioxidants are compounds that help repair the damage to your cells caused by free radicals â€” harmful molecules that cause oxidative stress. Oxidative stress is associated with several chronic health conditions, including diabetes, cancer, and heart disease. Grapes are rich in several powerful antioxidants. The highest concentration of antioxidants is found in the skin and seeds.
